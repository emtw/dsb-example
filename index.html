D.S.B. Welding & Sheet Metal Ltd. (formerly DOMIC) was established in 1979. Since then, we have built up a reputation for reliability, quality & competitiveness within the sheet metal & welding industries.

Our aims:

  • Build & develop long-term relationships with every customer.
  • Deliver quality workmanship.
  • Have a rapid turnaround & provide good value for money.
  • Provide a friendly & efficient service; making customer satisfaction our top priority.

We take great pride in our work; giving our customers precision engineering at the highest quality. With our continued investments in the latest technologies and machinery, we are able to offer our customers competitive prices and a speedy turnaround.

We encourage you to browse our website and feel free to make an enquiry. No matter what your project involves, with our fresh and flexible approach and forward thinking, we can collaborate to create the finished product.

Our specialist services include the following:

  • CNC Plasma Profiling
  • CNC Laser Cutting
  • CNC Press Brake
